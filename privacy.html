What we collect

This website (specstage.com) is a static information site. We collect two kinds of data:

  • Analytics — if you accept the cookie consent banner, Google Analytics 4 records anonymized usage data: pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referrer. We use this only to understand which pages people find useful. You can decline at any time and we will not load Google Analytics. We also use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-friendly and uses no cookies.
  • Email correspondence — if you email hello@specstage.com to request beta access, we keep your message and reply. We use this only to respond and to send occasional updates about SpecStage. We will not share your email with anyone.

What we do not collect

  • We do not track you across other sites.
  • We do not sell or share data with advertisers.

How the SpecStage application handles your project files

SpecStage does not operate a backend server. Project documents you load into the application — RFPs, design criteria, drawings, UFGS master sections — are stored locally on your machine and are not uploaded to or retained by SpecStage.

To generate tailoring recommendations, the application sends the minimum content needed for each decision (relevant text excerpts, project context, and the specification section under review) to its AI model provider, Anthropic. Anthropic's API terms specify that inputs sent through the API are not used to train their models.

All AI outputs are returned to your local machine for engineer review and approval before any export. Decision logs and tailored output files are written locally; we do not receive copies.

Cookies

Only one cookie category is used: Google Analytics, gated behind a consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set. A small localStorage entry remembers your choice so we don't ask again on every visit.

Your choices

You can:

  • Decline analytics at any time via the consent banner — clear your browser's site data for specstage.com to be re-prompted.
  • Email hello@specstage.com to request deletion of any correspondence we hold about you.
