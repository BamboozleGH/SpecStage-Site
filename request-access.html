Private beta

Apply for the SpecStage private beta.

SpecStage is currently accepting a small number of new users:

  • Federal construction engineers — USACE, NAVFAC, AFCEC
  • Specification writers and tailoring leads
  • A/E firms delivering federal projects

The application is short. If we're a good fit, we'll respond within a few business days.

Tell us about your work

Four short questions.

We use your answers to gauge fit and to make your first project go smoothly. Nothing is shared.

  1. What type of UFGS projects do you work on?
    Branch (USACE / NAVFAC / AFCEC / other), facility types, typical scope.
  2. How often do you tailor specifications?
    Per week, per month, per year — whatever frames it best.
  3. Do you currently use SpecsIntact?
    If yes, briefly — version, working directory pattern, anything notable about your workflow.
  4. One section you'd like to test with SpecStage.
    Pick something representative of your typical work — we'll prioritise it for your first run.
Open the email → Opens your email client with the four questions pre-filled. Or just write to hello@specstage.com.

What happens next

We read every application personally. If we're a good fit you'll receive a download link and a short onboarding walkthrough. If the timing isn't right, we'll tell you that too.

Privacy

We use your responses only to evaluate beta access and to follow up with you. We don't share them, sell them, or feed them into a CRM. See the full privacy policy.
